6 Facts about the Porsche 911

Time-tested design: The appearance of the model has remained virtually unchanged since 1964, retaining a recognizable silhouette.
Rear-engine layout: The Porsche 911, unlike most modern sports cars, retains the classic rear-engine layout.
Motorsport successes: More than two-thirds of Porsche's 30,000 motorsports victories were achieved with the 911 model of various generations and modifications.
Multiple modifications: The car is available in a variety of versions, including coupe, convertible, Targa and all-wheel drive options.
Innovative Technology: Despite its conservative design, the Porsche 911 has always been at the forefront of technology, including modern safety and performance systems.
Cultural influence: The 911 has become an icon of automotive culture, a symbol of status and success for many generations.
