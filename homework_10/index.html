Our vision ♥

The Fondation L'Oréal is committed to empowering women, enabling them to express their full potential, take control of their destiny, and make a positive impact on society, through two areas of intervention that are central to L'Oréal's commitments: scientific research and inclusive beauty. In today's world, persistent cultural biases and sexist stereotypes still prevent women from fully realizing their potential. Inequalities are widening and these obstacles are multiplying, also affecting their ability to contribute fully to a more just and sustainable world. This is why, since 2007 and more than ever, the Fondation L'Oréal has been mobilized, with and for women, so that they can take their rightful place and contribute to building a more inclusive and sustainable world.

Our mission ♥

The Fondation L’Oréal is committed to supporting women scientists at every stage of their careers, from sparking vocations at a young age to recognizing excellence in established researchers. This commitment translates into an intergenerational approach and dedicated programmes. In partnership with UNESCO, The Fondation L’Oréal has run the "For Women in Science" programme since 1998. This programme aims to accelerate the careers of women scientists, remove the obstacles they face, and inspire younger generations to embrace scientific careers. To date, the programme has supported more than 4,700 researchers from over 140 countries, promoting scientific excellence and encouraging many young women to pursue scientific studies. The Fondation L’Oréal also invests in the future of women in science through its "For Girls in Science" programme. This programme aims to inspire scientific vocations in young girls by raising their awareness of the scientific challenges of tomorrow and introducing them to inspiring female role models. The goal is to support and encourage the next generation of female scientists, giving them the keys to succeed in scientific fields.
